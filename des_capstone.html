ATTA

For my DVC project, I reimagined the Auckland Table Tennis Stadium, drawing inspiration from the timeless design principles of legendary architects such as Mies Van Der Rohe and Tadao Ando. Embracing Van Der Rohe's philosophy of "less is more," I incorporated clean lines, open spaces, and a minimalist aesthetic to create a sense of clarity and functionality. From Tadao Ando, I adopted the use of raw materials like concrete, and played around with the effects of light and shadow to evoke a sense of tranquility and timelessness.
